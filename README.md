## 🚀 The Founder Story — Grhams → Grhamly

In **2022** I started **Grhams** ([grhams.in](https://grhams.in)), an **interior-design firm**. I built a strong brand from scratch, developed relationships with suppliers, contractors, and professionals, and — with my co-founder (an interior designer) — delivered design projects on tight timelines and budgets, earning a reputation for reliability.

While running those projects, I spotted an opening in the **home-decor products** market — strong margins, room to grow. So I reinvested the firm's profits to launch **Grhamly** ([grhamly.com](https://grhamly.com)), a **D2C e-commerce brand** selling furniture, artifacts, vases, kitchenware, and bedsheets. I sourced from manufacturing units across India and built the **supply chain and vendor relationships** to back it.

Two ventures, one playbook: **branding · business development · project management · team leadership · problem-solving** — turning an idea into a running business, end to end.
